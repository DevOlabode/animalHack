# Elevator Pitch

**Phase:** MVP (Hackathon)

## 30-second

Vethra is a shared digital healthcare platform for pets. Pet owners create pet profiles, discover clinics, and book appointments with a clear reason for the visit. Clinics manage a calendar of pending, approved, and declined appointments, see the pet and issue before the visit, and record care over time.

## 60-second

Veterinary care is fragmented—paper records, lost vaccination cards, and incomplete histories. Vethra connects owners and clinics around each pet’s digital medical passport: appointments, diagnoses, prescriptions, reminders, and treatment compliance in one place. For AnimalHack we prove the core loop—discover → book → accept → care. After the hackathon we grow into a SaaS clinic operating system with reviews, staff accounts, social pet moments, articles, telemedicine, and analytics.

## Tagline options

- Care for every pet, in one timeline.
- Find a vet. Book a visit. Keep the record.
- The shared medical passport for pets.
