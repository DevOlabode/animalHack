# Problem Statement

## For pet owners

- Vaccination and medical records are lost or scattered across clinics.
- Booking a visit often means phone tag with little context for the vet.
- Medication and follow-up schedules are easy to forget.
- Switching clinics means starting over without history.

## For veterinary clinics

- Incomplete histories slow exams and increase risk.
- Paperwork and disconnected tools waste clinical time.
- No clear view of *why* a pet is coming until the animal is in the room.
- Follow-up compliance is hard to monitor after the visit.

## Result

Poor communication, inefficient care, lower treatment compliance, and worse outcomes for animals.

## Opportunity

A single web platform where owners describe the problem before booking, clinics triage and schedule with context, and both sides share one medical timeline after the visit.
