# Roadmap Phases

## Phase 0 — Hackathon MVP

Discover → book with reason → accept/decline → timeline → Rx → reminders.

## Phase 2 — Immediately after

- Reviews & ratings
- Rich clinic pages (values, team photos, structured hours)
- Geo / near-me search
- In-app notification center
- Appointment reschedule
- Staff accounts (multi-vet)
- Hardening: rate limits, email verification, Joi everywhere
- TanStack Query + design system cleanup
- Analytics basics for clinics

## Phase 3

- Subscription billing (clinic-paid)
- Advanced scheduling (waitlist, recurring)
- Record transfer between clinics
- SMS reminders
- Articles publishing

## Phase 4 — Future

- Social pet moments feed
- Telemedicine (video/chat)
- Mobile apps
- Insurance export helpers
- Wearables integrations
- AI assist (summaries — not autonomous diagnosis)
