# Monetization

Charge clinics; owners free initially.

| Plan | Price (indicative) | Includes |
|------|--------------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 1 vet, limited appointments |
| Starter | ~$29/mo | Scheduling + records |
| Pro | ~$79/mo | Unlimited + reminders + analytics |
| Enterprise | Custom | Multi-location, API, white-label |

## Why clinics pay

Less paperwork, better retention, faster history, follow-up compliance.
